The Sea

The breathtaking Sea here in Cordova

The seas provide habitats of variety of species. Seas are home to an incredible diversity of marine life, from microscopic organisms to large marine mammals. The sea also provides resources that supports human livelihoods.

Many families here in cordova are fisherman. Their main source of income are fish and other marine life on the sea.
